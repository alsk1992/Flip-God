# Analytics

Monitor your arbitrage business performance and discover trends.

## Commands

| Command | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| `daily report` | Today's summary (scans, listings, sales) |
| `profit dashboard` | Running P&L, margins, ROI |
| `top opportunities` | Best current arb opportunities |
| `category analysis` | Which categories have best margins |
| `competitor watch` | Track competitor pricing |
| `fee calculator` | Calculate platform fees |

## Examples

- "show me today's daily report"
- "what's my profit this week?"
- "show top 10 arbitrage opportunities with 20%+ margin"
- "analyze margins in the electronics category"
- "calculate eBay fees on a $49.99 item"

## Tips

- Review daily reports to spot trends
- Focus on categories with consistently high margins
- Watch competitor pricing to stay competitive
